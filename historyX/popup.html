Removes search engine query entries from your history.
  • google.com/search
  • bing.com/search
  • duckduckgo.com
  • search.yahoo.com/search
  • yandex.com/search
Only the search page itself is removed; links you click from the search results remain in your history.
You can add custom filters, which will automatically remove matching history entries. Filters can be specific or broad, for example:
  • store.example.com/products
  • example.com
  • example
Be careful: very broad filters may remove any history entry containing that term. Filters only apply to new history entries after they are set, not retroactively.
